Interactive Indoor Mapping

Customizable 2D and 3D indoor maps with real-time updates and AI-powered maintenance

How It Works

Our indoor mapping solution works by combining smartphone sensors with existing building infrastructure like WiFi and Bluetooth signals. We start by creating a detailed digital map of the space using advanced scanning techniques. Then, the app uses your phone's accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer to track movement in real-time, fusing this data with radio signals for pinpoint accuracy down to centimeters.

As you move, the system continuously calibrates and improves its positioning through machine learning, adapting to the environment. In a museum, it overlays interactive layers on the map for exhibits and crowds. For malls or campuses, it highlights stores, promotions, or available rooms, turning static navigation into a dynamic, user-friendly experience.

Key Features

  • Geo-referenced Mapping: Satellite-accurate positioning that integrates seamlessly with outdoor GPS for continuous navigation.
  • Custom Branding Options: Fully customizable visuals, from color schemes to branded icons, ensuring maps reflect your identity.
  • Multi-floor Visualization: Effortless switching between floors with 3D transitions, eliminating confusion in complex buildings.
  • Real-time Updates: Live data integration showing current events, crowds, or temporary closures.
  • AI-Powered Maintenance: Self-correcting maps that learn from user feedback and sensor data, staying accurate without manual updates.

Value Proposition

Interactive Indoor Mapping isn't just about getting from A to B, it's about creating immersive experiences that drive engagement and revenue. By providing accurate, branded maps, businesses can reduce wayfinding frustrations, increase time spent in-venue, and gather valuable location data. Studies show that effective indoor mapping can boost customer satisfaction by 40% and conversion rates by 25%.
