## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
1. Starting with emotional storytelling (Hospital)
2. Using business ROI for commercial pages (Mall, Casino)
3. Creating detailed strategy documents first
4. Committing frequently with clear messages
5. Working in focused sessions per page

### Challenges Overcome
1. Token budget management
2. Maintaining uniqueness across similar industries
3. Balancing technical accuracy with storytelling
4. Keeping consistent quality across all pages
5. Avoiding template language creep

### Best Practices Established
1. Lead with hook, not features
2. Use named testimonials, not generic quotes
3. Simple 3-tier pricing works best
4. Gradient CTAs perform better visually
5. Industry-specific stats more compelling than generic
