Platform Integration & Capabilities

Waide's theme park navigation platform integrates seamlessly with existing entertainment infrastructure through our three core solutions:

Smart Building Integration

Real-time crowd monitoring, environmental controls, and safety system integration for comprehensive park management. The platform tracks guest flow, manages lighting and climate systems, and provides emergency evacuation routing with real-time updates.

Proximity Advertising

Location-based notifications for dining, merchandise, and park services. Guests receive contextual information about nearby attractions, special offers, and personalized recommendations based on their location and visit history.

Geoconquesting

Competitive analysis and guest engagement strategies for entertainment venues. Track visitor patterns, optimize attraction layouts, and enhance guest experiences through data-driven insights and targeted marketing campaigns.
