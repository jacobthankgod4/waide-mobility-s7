The Library Navigation Challenge

Public knowledge centers with multi-floor collections witness patrons struggling to locate resources while managing complex multi-floor layouts, diverse collections, and growing digital resources. Research from the International Federation of Library Associations indicates that 68% of library visitors spend significant time searching for specific resources, with 34% requiring staff assistance for basic navigation tasks.

The challenge intensifies in large academic and public libraries where traditional Dewey Decimal organization meets modern digital integration requirements. Visitors struggle to locate specific books, study areas, computer terminals, and specialized collections across multiple floors and wings, leading to reduced library utilization and increased operational costs.

Digital Wayfinding: The Library Revolution

Leading libraries globally have embraced digital wayfinding as essential infrastructure for user engagement. The British Library reported 51% improvement in resource discovery efficiency and 38% increase in visitor satisfaction after implementing indoor navigation systems. Similar results at Stanford University Libraries and Singapore National Library demonstrate the transformative impact of technology-enabled library navigation.

Effective library navigation systems require integration with catalog systems, real-time availability updates, study space management, and accessibility features that serve users with diverse research needs and physical capabilities.

Smart library navigation helps visitors find books and resources efficiently

Waide's Library Navigation Solution

Waide Mobility addresses these library challenges through our comprehensive AI-powered indoor mapping platform. Our solution transforms existing library layouts into interactive, searchable digital maps that provide precise location services for books, resources, and facilities across complex multi-floor library systems.

The platform leverages advanced computer vision and machine learning to process library floor plans, converting them into geo-referenced digital maps with shelf-level accuracy. This enables precise navigation to specific call numbers, study areas, and specialized collections while integrating with existing library management systems.

Platform Integration & Capabilities

Waide's library navigation platform integrates seamlessly with existing infrastructure through our three core solutions:

Smart Building Integration

Real-time occupancy monitoring, environmental controls, and security system integration for comprehensive library management. The platform tracks study space utilization, manages lighting and climate systems, and provides emergency evacuation routing.

Proximity Advertising

Location-based notifications for library events, new acquisitions, and relevant resources. Users receive contextual information about nearby collections, upcoming workshops, and personalized recommendations based on their research interests.

Geoconquesting

Competitive analysis and user engagement strategies for academic libraries. Track visitor patterns, optimize resource placement, and enhance user experience through data-driven insights.
