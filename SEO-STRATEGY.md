   Answer: Waide Mobility provides seamless indoor-outdoor navigation using AI-powered technology that combines GPS for outdoor spaces with advanced indoor positioning systems. 2. **Can I create interactive indoor maps without coding?**
   Answer: Yes! Waide's platform at maps.waidemobility.org allows venue owners to upload floor plans and our AI automatically converts them into interactive indoor maps within 3 weeks. No coding required - just upload your floorplan, and our system handles digitization, georeferencing, and interactive feature creation.

3. **What is smart venue mobility?**
   Answer: Smart venue mobility refers to intelligent navigation systems that adapt to your venue's unique needs. Waide provides real-time wayfinding, accessibility features, crowd flow optimization, and integration with building management systems to create seamless visitor experiences in malls, airports, hospitals, and other complex spaces.

4. **How does station wayfinding work?**
   Answer: Station wayfinding uses indoor navigation technology to guide passengers through train stations, subway systems, and transit hubs. Waide's platform provides platform-specific directions, real-time transit updates, accessibility routing, and multi-language support to help travelers navigate complex transportation facilities.

5. **What is theme park wayfinding?**
   Answer: Theme park wayfinding helps visitors navigate attractions, find amenities, and optimize their visit. Waide's system provides attraction wait times, accessibility information, dining recommendations, and turn-by-turn navigation to enhance the guest experience in entertainment venues.
