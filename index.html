How It Works

1

Venue Floorplan Submission

Venues submit their floorplans, a crucial first step in building a map that enables accessible navigation and independence for everyone.

2

Digitization & Georeferencing

We digitize the floorplan and georeference it to Earth's coordinates using GIS indoor mapping tools. We then add points of interest and routing for seamless navigation.

3

App Integration

This digitized interactive floorplan is integrated into our app, providing users with accurate, turn-by-turn directions and a new level of confidence and freedom.

About Waide Mobility

Founded in Africa with a vision for inclusive navigation, Waide started by solving wayfinding challenges for the visually impaired using smartphones. Today, we expand that foundation to B2B, providing real-time location insights from global satellites. Our tech tracks movements accurately, enabling brands in retail, logistics, and hospitality to personalize services, optimize operations, and enhance customer loyalty. Whether it's guiding shoppers back from competitors or streamlining airport flows, Waide blends innovation with impact. Join us in building smarter, more connected worlds.

Our Mission

With smart AI technology, satellite tracking, and IoT tech, we make journeys from lively city streets to busy airports a breeze, helping brands figure out what customers want at the exact time, create tailored experiences, and win back loyal customers from rival stores, while letting everyone, especially individuals with visual impairments, move through these public spaces smoothly with easy-to-use accessible tools.
