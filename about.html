Our Story

Pioneering the Future of
Accessible Mobility

Founded in 2019, Waide Mobility emerged from a simple vision: to make navigation intuitive and transportation intelligent for everyone across the African continent. We're designed to serve everyone, including the 250 million people with disabilities in Africa who need to seamlessly navigate indoor spaces through cutting-edge AI and human-centered design.

Our Mission

To revolutionize accessible mobility by creating intelligent, sustainable, and inclusive transportation ecosystems that connect communities, empower people of all abilities, and reduce environmental impact through cutting-edge AI, immersive engagements, IoT integration, and data-driven insights.

Our Vision

A world where every journey is seamless, every space is intelligently connected, and sustainable transportation is accessible to all. We envision smart cities where mobility flows effortlessly, powered by AI and human-centered design, creating inclusive environments for everyone.

Our Journey

From a chance encounter at a conference to a platform serving millions across Africa

2019 - The Vision

Foundation & Early Concept

Waide Mobility was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to make navigation intuitive and accessible for everyone, everywhere. Our initial focus was on understanding the unique challenges faced by people with disabilities in navigating complex indoor environments.

2023 - The Breakthrough

Inclusive Africa Conference

The pivotal moment came at the 2023 Inclusive Africa Conference in Nairobi, where our founder Jacob witnessed firsthand the navigation challenges faced by visually impaired individuals. This experience crystallized our mission and led to the development of our core technology.

2025 - Scaling Impact

Platform Launch & Growth

Today, Waide Mobility is designed to serve everyone across the entire African continent, including the 250 million people with disabilities who need seamless indoor navigation. We are working towards expanding our team globally and established partnerships across multiple countries to maximize our continental impact.

Our Technology

Cutting-edge AI and AR technologies that transform smartphones into powerful accessibility tools

AI-Powered Navigation

Advanced machine learning algorithms that provide real-time, context-aware navigation guidance tailored to individual user needs and preferences.

Augmented Reality

Immersive AR experiences that overlay digital navigation guides onto the real world, creating intuitive wayfinding solutions for complex indoor spaces.

Climate-Resilient Design

Offline-capable technology optimized for low-resource environments, ensuring reliable navigation during extreme weather events and connectivity challenges.

Universal Accessibility

WCAG-compliant interfaces with multi-language support, voice commands, and seamless integration with assistive technologies for truly inclusive experiences.
