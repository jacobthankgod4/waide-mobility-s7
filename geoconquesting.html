Geoconquesting

Precision marketing that intercepts customers near competitors and guides them to your venue

How It Works

Our geoconquesting system uses precise indoor and outdoor positioning to detect when users are near competitor locations. By integrating GPS, WiFi, and Bluetooth signals, it identifies proximity in real-time and triggers targeted campaigns. Machine learning analyzes user behavior to deliver personalized offers, such as discounts or promotions, directly to their device with one-tap navigation to your venue.

In retail areas, it intercepts shoppers near rivals with exclusive deals; in food districts, it sends reservation incentives; at events, it offers last-minute tickets. This data-driven approach maximizes conversions by timing interventions at the moment of decision, while providing analytics on campaign effectiveness and competitor traffic patterns.

Key Features

  • Competitor Proximity Detection: Real-time geofencing around rival locations to trigger campaigns instantly.
  • Personalized Notifications: Tailored messages based on user behavior, preferences, and past interactions.
  • Seamless Navigation Integration: One-tap directions from detection point to your venue, reducing drop-off.
  • Multi-channel Delivery: Push notifications, in-app messages, SMS, and email for maximum reach.
  • Performance Analytics: Track interception rates, conversion lift, and ROI from each campaign.

Value Proposition

Geoconquesting transforms competition into opportunity, potentially increasing foot traffic by 30-50% during peak hours. By intercepting customers at the moment of decision, businesses can capture market share that would otherwise go to rivals, while gathering valuable data on competitor performance and customer preferences.

Benefits
