Geoconquesting

Precision marketing that intercepts customers near competitors and guides them to your venue

How It Works

Our geoconquesting system uses precise indoor and outdoor positioning to detect when users are near competitor locations. By integrating GPS, WiFi, and Bluetooth signals, it identifies proximity in real-time and triggers targeted campaigns. Machine learning analyzes user behavior to deliver personalized offers, such as discounts or promotions, directly to their device with one-tap navigation to your venue.

In retail areas, it intercepts shoppers near rivals with exclusive deals; in food districts, it sends reservation incentives; at events, it offers last-minute tickets. This data-driven approach maximizes conversions by timing interventions at the moment of decision, while providing analytics on campaign effectiveness and competitor traffic patterns.

Geoconquesting (Competitor Tracking through Geofencing)

Track competitors' locations and target customers in their vicinity with geofencing to gain competitive advantage and increase market share.

  • Competitor Proximity Detection: Real-time geofencing around rival locations to trigger campaigns instantly.

  • Multi-channel Delivery: Push notifications, in-app messages, SMS, and email for maximum reach.
  • Performance Analytics: Track interception rates, conversion lift, and ROI from each campaign.

How It Works

Imagine turning every competitor's location into your sales opportunity. Geoconquesting uses advanced geofencing to create invisible "capture zones" around rival businesses. When a potential customer enters these zones, perhaps browsing in a competitor's store or parking lot, our AI-powered system instantly identifies them and delivers personalized offers via push notifications, SMS, or in-app messages, steering them toward your business instead.

For instance, if a customer is shopping at a rival electronics store, they might receive a notification: "Get 20% off at our store just 2 blocks away, plus free delivery!" This intercepts the sale at the critical moment, converting competitor traffic into your revenue.

Key Features

  • Competitor Geofencing: Define precise virtual boundaries around any competitor location for proactive customer interception.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Monitor customer movement in real-time with privacy-compliant location data, triggering instant marketing actions.
  • Market Share Expansion: Capture high-intent buyers at the point of purchase, potentially increasing your market share by 15-25%.
  • Customizable Campaigns: Create dynamic promotions tailored to user profiles, location history, and competitor context.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Measure campaign effectiveness with metrics like interception rate, conversion lift, and incremental revenue.

Value Proposition

Geoconquesting isn't just about poaching customers, it's about dominating your market. By intelligently redirecting competitor traffic, businesses can achieve measurable ROI with campaigns that pay for themselves through increased sales. In competitive industries like retail and hospitality, this can mean the difference between growth and stagnation.
